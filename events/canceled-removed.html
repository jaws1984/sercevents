Teachers College Saturday Reunion - New York City Bus Trip To Columbia University

October 22, 2016

Leave the driving to us and join SERC on the bus as we travel to Teachers College for a day of professional development for literacy educators! The cost includes round-trip transportation and light snacks.

Two Saturdays a year, the Teachers College Reading and Writing Project (TCRWP) hosts a free day of professional learning for educators from all over the country. Saturday Reunions offer keynotes and workshops by leading educators, including TCRWP senior project staff and staff developers.

No registration fee is required by Teachers College to attend the Saturday Reunion Conference.
