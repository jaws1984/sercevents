2016-2017 CSDE Live Alternate Assessment System Training - Session A

September 29, 2016

This training is required for teachers who have primary responsibility for assessing students identified with significant disabilities and administering the CT Alternate Assessment (CTAA) and the CT Mastery Test (CMT)/CT Academic Performance Test (CAPT) Skills Checklist Science. This requirement does not apply to teachers who participated in the 2014-15 CSDE Live or Online CTAA & CMT/CAPT Skills Checklist Science Training.

As a result of this training, participants will demonstrate their understanding of the following: the identification process for the CTAA and CMT/CAPT Skills Checklist Science; the alignment between Connecticut’s Core Standards and the CTAA; and the CTAA and CMT/CAPT Skills Checklist Science procedures, timeline, administration, accommodations, and resources.

This training will be conducted in either of two half-day sessions in each of the six regional educational service center (RESC) areas between September 2016 and January 2017.

Time: 8:30 a.m.
